## RECOMMENDED PERMIT SEQUENCE

```
Week 1-2:   Submit Permit 1 (Remodel + Plumbing) — no structural, faster review
Week 1-2:   Submit Permit 3 (Electrical) — contact utility for 400A service concurrently
Week 2-4:   Engage structural engineer; prepare Permit 2 drawings
Week 4-6:   Submit Permit 2 (Structural + Exterior)

Permit 1 approval → start interior demo, re-pipe, bathroom rough-in
Permit 3 approval → panel upgrade, rough electrical
Permit 2 approval → structural demo, headers, exterior doors, concrete
```
