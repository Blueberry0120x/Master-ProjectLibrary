## SECTION 4 — PERMIT 1 SCOPE (Interior Remodel + Plumbing)

**Description of Work:**

Interior residential remodel of existing single-family dwelling at 907 Orchid Way, Carlsbad CA 92011. Scope includes:

- **Bathrooms (Master + Hall):** Remove existing tile walls and install cement board substrate throughout. Replace existing tub/shower with new walk-in tile shower including new shower pan and drain. Replace existing floor-mounted toilets with wall-mounted toilets (new in-wall carrier frames). Install new owner-supplied cabinetry. New tile flooring throughout both bathrooms. Add electrical outlets near toilets (for bidet) and under sinks.
- **Powder Room (New):** Add new half-bath (toilet + lavatory) at location per approved plans. New plumbing rough-in: 1 toilet supply/drain, 1 lavatory supply/drain, new vent connection to existing vent stack.
- **Kitchen:** Plumbing rough-in for sink and dishwasher relocation per approved plans. Owner to supply and install cabinets and appliances.
- **Attic:** Remove and replace attic insulation (spray or batt, per Title 24). Install ¾" plywood sheathing over attic joists for storage. Install new drop-down attic access staircase.
- **Flooring:** Remove existing flooring throughout entire dwelling and install new tile (owner-supplied). Includes all living areas, hallways, bedrooms, kitchen, and bathrooms.
- **Plumbing — Whole House Re-Pipe:** Replace all existing supply piping throughout dwelling with PEX-A. Includes all hot and cold supply lines to all fixtures, water heater, hose bibs, and appliances. Existing DWV (drain/waste/vent) to remain unless noted otherwise.
- **Water Heater:** Replace existing water heater with new electric water heater at existing or approved location. New 240V dedicated circuit by others (Electrical Permit).
