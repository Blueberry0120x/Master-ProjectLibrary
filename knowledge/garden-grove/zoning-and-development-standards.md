Source: Garden Grove Municipal Code (GGMC) Title 10 — Planning and Zoning.
Online: https://www.codepublishing.com/CA/GardenGrove/

## 1. Base Zone Classifications

Garden Grove organizes base zones into residential, commercial, industrial, and special categories.

### Residential Zones

| Zone | Name | Typical Min. Lot | Base Density |
|------|------|-----------------|--------------|
| RS-1 | Single-Family Residential (Large Lot) | 10,000 sf | 1 du/lot |
| RS-2 | Single-Family Residential (Medium Lot) | 7,200 sf | 1 du/lot |
| RS-3 | Single-Family Residential (Small Lot) | 6,000 sf | 1 du/lot |
| RS-4 | Single-Family Residential (Urban) | 5,000 sf | 1 du/lot |
| RM-1 | Multiple-Family Residential (Low) | 6,000 sf | Up to 12 du/ac |
| RM-2 | Multiple-Family Residential (Medium) | 6,000 sf | Up to 24 du/ac |
| RM-3 | Multiple-Family Residential (High) | 6,000 sf | Up to 40 du/ac |

**Always verify zone on GardenGrove.org zoning maps — parcel-specific overlays can modify standards.**

## 2. Residential Development Standards (Single-Family)

### Setbacks — RS-2 (most common SFD zone)

| Setback | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| Front yard | 20 ft (measured from front property line) |
| Side yard (interior) | 5 ft |
| Side yard (street) | 10 ft |
| Rear yard | 20% of lot depth, minimum 15 ft |

**Verify exact setbacks for your zone in GGMC Title 10.** Standards vary by RS zone and can
be modified by overlay districts (Specific Plans, Historic Districts).

### Building Height

| Zone | Maximum Height |
|------|---------------|
| RS-1 through RS-4 | 28 ft to peak; 2 stories max |
| RM-1 | 35 ft; 3 stories max |
| RM-2 | 45 ft |
| RM-3 | 55 ft |

### Lot Coverage

| Zone | Max Lot Coverage |
|------|-----------------|
| RS-1 | 40% |
| RS-2 | 45% |
| RS-3 | 50% |
| RS-4 | 55% |
| RM zones | Varies by zone — check GGMC |

### Floor Area Ratio (FAR)

Garden Grove does not universally apply FAR to single-family residential in the same
manner as San Diego. Development standards focus on lot coverage, height, and setbacks.
For commercial zones, FAR limits apply per GGMC Title 10.

## 3. Parking Requirements

### Residential Parking

| Use | Required Spaces |
|-----|----------------|
| SFD (1–3 bedrooms) | 2 spaces, at least 1 in garage |
| SFD (4+ bedrooms) | 2 spaces in garage |
| ADU (≤ 500 sf or studio/1BR) | 0 additional (state law) |
| ADU (> 500 sf) | 1 space per unit |
| Multifamily (1 BR) | 1 space/unit + 0.25 guest |
| Multifamily (2+ BR) | 2 spaces/unit + 0.5 guest |

**State ADU law supersedes local parking requirements for ADUs within ½ mile of transit.**

### Garage Dimensions

- Minimum clear interior: 20 ft deep × 20 ft wide (2-car garage)
- Single-car garage: 10 ft × 20 ft clear interior minimum

## 4. Accessory Structures

| Standard | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|

| Setback from rear | 5 ft minimum |
| Setback from side | 5 ft minimum |
| Lot coverage | Counts toward overall max lot coverage |
| Permit required | Yes — any structure > 120 sf or attached to main structure |

## 5. Overlay Districts & Special Plans

Garden Grove has several overlay districts that can modify base zone standards:

- **Historic Preservation Overlay** — Properties on the Historic Register or in
  a historic district require design review; demolition triggers CEQA analysis.
- **Town Center Specific Plan** — Downtown/civic core; mixed-use; specific
  setbacks, stepbacks, design guidelines.
- **Chapman Avenue Corridor** — Commercial corridor with enhanced design standards.
- **Brookhurst Community Plan** — Mixed-use corridor; height and density bonuses
  for affordable housing.
- **West Garden Grove Specific Plan** — Industrial transition area.

**Always check for applicable overlay on the Planning Division's zoning map.**

---

## 6. Density Bonus (State Law — Gov Code 65915)

- Applies city-wide for qualifying affordable projects.
- Base density for bonus calculation uses the applicable zone density.
- Garden Grove must grant density bonus + at least one incentive/concession
  per state law (no local findings needed).
- ADUs can count as density-bonus units in multifamily contexts.

## 7. Sign Code Summary

Signs regulated by GGMC Title 10, Sign Chapter. Key limits for residential/small commercial:
- Monument signs: max 6 ft height, 32 sf per face for commercial.
- No illuminated signs in RS zones.
- Temporary construction signs: max 32 sf, remove within 7 days of final inspection.

---

## 8. Environmental Review

- **CEQA** applies to all discretionary approvals (CUP, variance, zone change, tentative map).
- Most ministerial SFD/ADU permits are **categorically exempt** (Class 3 — New Construction).
- Projects in flood zones (FEMA FIRM), biological resource areas, or historic districts
  may require Initial Study / Mitigated Negative Declaration.
