## 1. Permit Types

| Permit Type | Trigger | Notes |
|-------------|---------|-------|
| Building Permit — New Construction | New SFD, ADU, commercial building | Requires plan check |
| Building Permit — Addition/Alteration | Additions > 120 sf, structural changes | Requires plan check |
| Mechanical Permit | HVAC, exhaust systems | May be issued over counter |
| Electrical Permit | New service, panel upgrade, EV charger | May be issued over counter |
| Plumbing Permit | Drain/waste/vent, water service changes | May be issued over counter |
| Grading Permit | Cut/fill > 50 cy or > 1 ft depth | City Public Works review |
| Demolition Permit | Full or partial structure demolition | Asbestos survey required > 100 sf |
| Solar Permit | PV system installation | Streamlined per SB 379; OTC for qualifying systems |

## 2. Plan Check Submittal Requirements

### Residential New Construction (SFD / ADU)

**Minimum exhibit set:**

1. **Site Plan** (1:20 or 1 in = 10 ft) — property lines, setbacks, easements, street names,
   North arrow, existing/proposed structures, utilities, drainage flow arrows
2. **Floor Plans** — all levels, room labels, dimensions, door/window schedule
3. **Elevations** — all four exterior elevations, finish materials labeled
4. **Sections** — minimum one building section showing floor-to-floor and roof structure
5. **Foundation Plan** — slab/footing details, soil bearing capacity per soils report or waiver
6. **Roof Plan** — drainage, skylights, solar rough-in if applicable
7. **Structural Drawings** — if engineered (shear walls, moment frames, hold-downs)
8. **Title 24 Energy Compliance (CF1R)** — generated by compliance software
9. **CALGreen Checklist** — residential mandatory measures
10. **Construction & Demolition Debris Management Plan (CDDMP)** — see `calgreen-energy.md`
11. **Truss Plans** (if manufactured trusses) — deferred submittal with truss engineering letter

### Over-the-Counter (OTC) Plan Check

Garden Grove Building Division offers same-day OTC plan check for:
- Detached accessory structures ≤ 500 sf
- Simple additions ≤ 500 sf, no new bedrooms
- Roof replacements and re-roofs
- Water heater replacements
- Solar PV systems meeting SB 379 streamlined criteria

### ePlans / Electronic Submittal

Verify with Building Division whether ePlans (electronic plan submittal) is currently
accepted. Garden Grove has been transitioning to digital workflows — call (714) 741-5165
to confirm current requirements.

## 3. Plan Check Process

```
1. Pre-submittal (optional)  →  Call Building Division for complex projects
2. Initial submittal          →  Submit plan set + application + fees at counter
                                  or electronically
3. First plan check           →  Typically 10–15 business days (SFD); 20–30 days (larger)
4. Corrections issued         →  Written correction list; respond item-by-item
5. Resubmittal                →  Submit revised plans + response letter
6. Second/Final plan check    →  5–10 business days (minor corrections)
7. Permit issuance            →  Pick up permit, post on job site
8. Inspections                →  Request via phone or online portal
9. Final inspection           →  All trades sign off; Certificate of Occupancy issued
```

**Expedited plan check:** Garden Grove offers fee-based expedited review (typically
50% premium). Request at submittal. Turnaround approx. 5 business days.

## 4. Fees

Fee schedule published annually by Community Development Dept.

| Fee Type | Basis |
|----------|-------|
| Plan Check Fee | % of building valuation (typically 65–75% of permit fee) |
| Building Permit Fee | Valuation table per ICC building valuation table |
| Mechanical / Electrical / Plumbing | Flat fee per unit or system |
| Grading | Per cubic yard of earthwork |
| Energy Code | Flat fee (added to building permit) |

**Current fee schedule:** Obtain from Building Division counter or city website.

## 5. Required Inspections

| Inspection | Timing |
|------------|--------|
| Foundation / Pre-pour | Before concrete; after forms and rebar set |
| Underground Plumbing | Before backfill |
| Frame | Before insulation or drywall; all framing, blocking, anchors exposed |
| Insulation | Before drywall |
| Drywall Nailing / Sheathing | Before tape and texture |
| Rough Mechanical / Electrical / Plumbing | During frame stage |
| Energy — Insulation | Before drywall (HERS if required) |
| Final — Building | All work complete; prior to occupancy |
| Final — Mechanical / Electrical / Plumbing | All systems complete |
| Final — Fire (if GGFD review required) | Fire systems complete |

**Request inspections:** Call Building Division inspection line or use online portal.
48-hour advance notice typically required.

---

## 6. Certificate of Occupancy

- Issued after all final inspections are signed off.
- Required before occupancy of any new structure or change of use.
- ADUs: separate C/O required for each ADU unit.

---

## 7. Common Reasons for Corrections

- Missing or incomplete energy compliance (CF1R not matching plans)
- CALGreen checklist incomplete or inconsistent with plan notes
- CDDMP not submitted or missing tonnage calculation
- Structural details missing (hold-downs, shear panel schedule)
- Site plan missing setback dimensions or easements
- Fire sprinkler notes absent (GGFD requires NFPA 13D for new SFD)
- Parking count not meeting GGMC requirements
