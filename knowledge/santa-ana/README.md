# City of Santa Ana — Plan-Check Reference Library

Master jurisdictional reference for City of Santa Ana permit submittals.
Scope: Single-family (SFD), ADU/JADU, additions, multifamily, and small commercial.
Santa Ana is the county seat of Orange County, CA — dense urban infill city.

Code cycle baseline: **2022 California codes** (CBC/CRC/CPC/CMC/CEC/CALGreen/CFC)
as locally amended per Santa Ana Municipal Code (SAMC).
**2025 cycle effective Jan 1, 2026.**

## Global Notes / Gotchas

- **Dense urban context.** Santa Ana is one of the most densely populated cities in CA.
  Lot sizes are often small (< 5,000 sf); setbacks and FAR limits are tight.
- **Specific Plan areas.** The Downtown Specific Plan, South Main Specific Plan, and
  Harbor Mixed-Use Transit Corridor overlay portions of the city with different standards.
  Always check for Specific Plan coverage on your parcel.
- **Affordable housing programs.** Santa Ana has active density bonus and inclusionary
  programs; ADU and SB 9 projects often intersect with housing opportunity programs.
- **Fire sprinklers.** SAFD requires NFPA 13D for new SFD and ADU construction per local
  amendment. Confirm current trigger threshold at (714) 647-5720.
- **2025 code cycle.** Verify with Building Safety which cycle applies to your submittal date.
