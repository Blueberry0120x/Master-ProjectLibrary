## 1. Permit Types

| Permit Type | Trigger | Notes |
|-------------|---------|-------|
| Building Permit — New Construction | New SFD, ADU, multifamily, commercial | Full plan check required |
| Building Permit — Addition/Alteration | Additions > 120 sf, structural changes | Plan check required |
| Mechanical Permit | HVAC, exhaust, fuel-burning equipment | May be OTC for standard work |
| Electrical Permit | New service, panel upgrade, EV charger | May be OTC |
| Plumbing Permit | Drain/waste/vent, water service | May be OTC |
| Grading Permit | Cut/fill > 50 cy or > 1 ft; slopes | Engineering Division review |
| Demolition Permit | Full or partial demolition | Asbestos survey required |
| Solar PV Permit | PV system installation | Streamlined OTC per SB 379 |
| Fire Sprinkler Permit | NFPA 13/13D/13R installation | SAFD review — typically deferred |
| Right-of-Way Permit | Work in public ROW | Public Works Engineering |

## 2. Plan Check Submittal Requirements

### Residential New Construction (SFD / ADU)

**Minimum exhibit set:**

1. **Site Plan** (≤ 1 in = 20 ft) — property lines, setbacks, easements, street names,
   North arrow, all structures, drainage arrows, utilities
2. **Floor Plans** — dimensioned, room labels, door/window schedule
3. **Elevations** — all four exterior faces, materials labeled
4. **Sections** — minimum one building section
5. **Foundation Plan** — slab/footing details
6. **Structural Plans + Calculations** — if engineered
7. **Roof Plan** — drainage, PV rough-in if applicable
8. **Title 24 Energy CF1R** — from CEC-approved compliance software
9. **CALGreen Residential Checklist** — mandatory measures
10. **C&D Waste Management Plan** — ≥ 65% diversion
11. **Stormwater BMP Site Plan** — standard project BMPs at minimum
12. **Truss Plans** — deferred submittal with engineer of record letter

### Over-the-Counter (OTC) Plan Check

Santa Ana Building Safety offers OTC plan check for:
- Simple residential alterations (no new bedrooms, no structural changes)
- Roof replacements
- Water heater and HVAC replacements
- Solar PV systems (SB 379 qualifying)
- Small accessory structures ≤ 500 sf

## 3. Plan Check Process

```
1. Pre-submittal consultation (recommended for ADU, new SFD)
2. Submittal — counter or online portal
3. Application accepted + fees collected
4. Intake review (completeness) — 1–5 biz days
5. Discipline routing — Building, Zoning, Fire, Stormwater, Engineering
6. Cycle 1 plan check — 15–25 biz days (SFD/ADU); 20–40 biz days (MF/commercial)
7. Consolidated correction list issued
8. Applicant response — revised plans + written response
9. Cycle 2 — 10–15 biz days
10. Permit ready — pay balance, school fees, impact fees
11. Permit issued — post on site
12. Inspections — by trade and building
13. Final inspection → Certificate of Occupancy
```

**Expedited plan check:** Available for fee premium (typically 50%). Request at submittal.

## 4. Required Inspections

| Inspection | Timing |
|------------|--------|
| Foundation / Pre-pour | After forms, rebar, sonotube set; before concrete |
| Underground Plumbing | Before backfill |
| Frame | All framing, shear nailing, blocking, anchors exposed |
| Insulation | Before drywall |
| Drywall Nailing | Before taping/finishing |
| Rough Mechanical / Electrical / Plumbing | During frame stage |
| Energy — Insulation | Before drywall (HERS if required) |
| Final Building | All work complete |
| Final Mechanical / Electrical / Plumbing | All systems complete |
| Final Fire | SAFD sign-off if fire review required |

**Request inspections:** Online portal, automated phone line, or in person.
Typically 48-hour advance notice required.

## 5. Fees

| Fee Type | Basis |

## 6. Common Correction Reasons

- Missing or incomplete Title 24 CF1R / energy notes on plans
- CALGreen checklist missing or not coordinated with plan notes
- C&D Waste Management Plan not submitted
- Stormwater BMP site plan absent
- Structural details missing (hold-downs, shear panel schedule)
- Fire sprinkler notes absent or inconsistent (SAFD requirement)
- Setbacks not dimensioned on site plan
- Parking count not meeting SAMC requirements
