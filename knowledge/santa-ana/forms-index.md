# Santa Ana — Forms & Submittals Index

All forms available from Planning & Building Agency — Building Safety Division
unless noted. (714) 647-5800 | https://www.santa-ana.org/pba/building-safety

---

## Permit Application Forms

| Form | Name | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Building Permit Application | Standard permit application | Counter or online portal |
| Owner-Builder Declaration | When owner acts as GC | No licensed contractor |
| Contractor Disclosure | Lists trade contractors | Submit with application |

## CALGreen / C&D Forms

| Form | Name | When Required |
|------|------|--------------|
| C&D Waste Management Plan | Construction waste diversion plan — ≥ 65% | All new construction + demolition |
| C&D Completion Form | Post-construction diversion documentation | Submit with hauler receipts at close |
| CALGreen Residential Checklist | Mandatory measures (Part 11 Ch 4) | Attach to plan check submittal |
| CALGreen Non-Residential Checklist | Mandatory measures (Part 11 Ch 5) | Non-residential projects |

## Notes

- **No WMF.** Santa Ana uses a C&D Waste Management Plan, not San Diego's WMF.
- **Diversion target:** 65% by weight per CALGreen 4.408.
- Specific Plan areas (Downtown, Harbor, South Main) may require additional
  design review forms — confirm with Planning Division.
