# Santa Ana — Fire Code & SAFD Requirements

Source: SAMC Title II Ch 9 (local CFC amendments) + 2022 CFC (Title 24 Part 9).
Santa Ana Fire Department (SAFD): (714) 647-5720 | https://www.santa-ana.org/fire

---

## 1. Applicable Codes

| Code | Applicability |
|------|--------------|
| 2022 California Fire Code (CFC) | Statewide baseline |
| SAMC Title II, Ch 9 | Local CFC amendments |
| NFPA 13D | SFD residential sprinklers |
| NFPA 13R | Multifamily up to 4 stories |
| NFPA 13 | Full suppression (> 4-story or commercial) |
| NFPA 72 | Fire alarm and detection |

---

## 2. Fire Sprinkler Requirements

### Single-Family Dwellings

SAFD requires **NFPA 13D fire sprinklers for all new SFD construction** per local
amendment. This is consistent with OC-area standard practice.

- **ADU:** New detached ADU — confirm trigger with SAFD at (714) 647-5720.
  Many CA cities require sprinklers if the ADU exceeds a certain size threshold.
- **Remodels/additions:** Generally no sprinkler trigger unless addition exceeds 50%
  of existing structure value — verify with SAFD.

### Multifamily

- NFPA 13R for R-2 up to 4 stories.
- NFPA 13 for buildings > 4 stories or mixed-use with commercial podium.
- Deferred submittal to SAFD after building permit.

---

## 3. Fire Alarm & Detection

| Occupancy | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| SFD (new) | Smoke alarms per CRC R314 — hardwired, battery backup, interconnected |
| ADU | Smoke alarms per CRC R314; CO detector if gas or attached garage |
| R-2 multifamily | NFPA 72 fire alarm system in common areas; detectors in each unit |

---

## 4. Fire Access & Addressing

- Address numbers: minimum 4 in, visible from street, contrasting color.
- ADU requires separate address or unit number posted at entrance.
- Fire apparatus access: 20 ft clear width, 13 ft 6 in vertical clearance.
- Dead ends > 150 ft: approved turnaround required.
- Gates: Knox key switch or Knox Box.

### Fire Hydrant

- New SFD: hydrant within 600 ft (measured along road).
- Minimum 1,000 gpm flow.

---

## 5. Wildland-Urban Interface (WUI)

Santa Ana is a fully urbanized city. The majority of parcels are **not in a
VHFHSZ** — but always verify on CAL FIRE's FHSZ map for the specific parcel.

CAL FIRE FHSZ: https://www.fire.ca.gov/what-we-do/fire-prevention/fire-hazard-severity-zones

If WUI applies: Chapter 7A ignition-resistant construction + 100 ft defensible space.

---

## 6. SAFD Plan Review Process

1. Building Division routes plans to SAFD concurrent with building plan check.
2. SAFD reviews access, hydrants, sprinkler/alarm notes.
3. Corrections issued as part of consolidated report.
4. Sprinkler/alarm: separate deferred submittal to SAFD.
5. Final fire inspection before Certificate of Occupancy.
