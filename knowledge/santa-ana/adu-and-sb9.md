# Santa Ana — ADU & SB 9

Source: California Gov Code §65852.2 + Santa Ana Municipal Code Title IV (Ch 41).
State ADU law overrides conflicting local provisions — state law governs when in doubt.

---

## 1. State ADU Law Floor

All California jurisdictions comply with state ADU law as a minimum.
See `../_shared/statewide-adu-law.md` for full state-law coverage.

Key state minimums:
- **Ministerial approval** — no discretionary review, no CEQA, no public hearing.
- **By-right on any SFD or multifamily lot** in residential or mixed-use zone.
- **JADU:** up to 500 sf, within existing primary structure, one per lot.
- **Detached ADU:** up to 1,200 sf (or 50% of primary dwelling sf).
- **Attached ADU:** up to 50% of primary dwelling area, max 1,200 sf.
- **Setbacks:** 4 ft rear and side for new detached ADU; 0 ft for conversions.
- **Parking:** Not required within ½ mile of transit or for conversions.
- **65-day approval deadline** from complete application.

## 2. Santa Ana Local ADU Standards

### Permitted Locations

- SF and SF-A zones — one ADU + one JADU per lot with existing or proposed SFD.
- MF zones — state law allows ADUs; multifamily bonus ADU rules apply.
- Specific Plan areas — check individual Specific Plan for ADU provisions;
  most allow ministerial ADU per state law.

### ADU Size Limits

| Type | Maximum Size |
|------|-------------|
| JADU | 500 sf |
| Attached ADU | 50% of primary dwelling sf, max 1,200 sf |
| Detached ADU | 1,200 sf |

### Setbacks

| Type | Setback |
|------|---------|
| New detached ADU — rear | 4 ft minimum |
| New detached ADU — side | 4 ft minimum |
| Conversion of existing space | May retain nonconforming setback |

### Height

- Detached ADU within 4 ft of rear/side: max 16 ft.
- ADU over garage: up to 24 ft if base zone allows.

### Parking

- Not required within ½ mile of transit (Santa Ana has multiple OC bus lines and
  is on the OC streetcar corridor — verify TPA status for specific parcel).
- Not required for garage/space conversions.
- If required: 1 space (tandem in driveway permitted).

### Objective Design Standards

Santa Ana may apply objective design standards (AB 2221). Verify current standards
with Planning at (714) 647-5804. Typically include:
- Exterior materials compatible with primary dwelling.
- Entrance not through primary dwelling interior.
- Separate utilities metering (for detached ADU).

---

## 3. Fire Sprinklers for ADUs

SAFD requires NFPA 13D sprinklers for new ADU construction when the primary
dwelling is required to be sprinklered. Confirm current threshold with SAFD at
(714) 647-5720 — this requirement varies by year of adoption and local amendment.

---

## 4. Bonus ADUs (Multifamily)

On multifamily lots (MF-1, MF-2, MF-3):
- Up to 2 detached ADUs per lot (by-right, ministerial).
- Conversion ADUs = 25% of existing units (rounded down).

## 5. SB 9 — Two-Unit / Urban Lot Split

Gov Code §§65852.21 and 66411.7 (effective Jan 1, 2022).

### Two-Unit Development (65852.21)

- 2 primary units on any SFD lot in urbanized area.
- Both units may have ADU + JADU → up to 4 units total.
- Ministerial approval; no discretionary review.
- Setbacks: 4 ft rear and side.
- Exclusions: historic landmarks, floodplains, farmland, VHFHSZ, wetlands, slopes > 25%.

### Urban Lot Split (66411.7)

- Split SFD parcel into two parcels; minimum 1,200 sf each.
- Tentative Parcel Map required; ministerial.
- Owner-occupancy required for 3 years (lot split only).
- Each new lot eligible for 2 units + ADU + JADU.

**Santa Ana urban lot splits are particularly active** given the city's density and
small lot stock. Confirm parcel is not in a historic district before advising client.

## 6. Common Issues in Santa Ana

- **Specific Plan conflicts.** Downtown and Harbor corridor Specific Plans may have
  additional design requirements for ADUs that go beyond base zone standards. Confirm
  with Planning whether Specific Plan design review applies.
- **Historic districts.** French Park, Heninger Park, and other historic residential
  neighborhoods require compatibility review for ADUs that alter the historic structure.
- **School fees.** ADUs > 500 sf: SAUSD Level 1 school fees apply. Contact SAUSD
  at (714) 558-5501.
- **Utility connections.** Separate water/sewer service for detached ADU requires
  Public Works Engineering review. Capacity fees may apply.
