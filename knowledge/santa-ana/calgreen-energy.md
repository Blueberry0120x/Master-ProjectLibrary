# Santa Ana — CALGreen & Energy

Code baseline: 2022 CALGreen (Title 24 Part 11) + 2022 Title 24 Part 6 (Energy Code).
2025 cycle effective Jan 1, 2026. No confirmed Santa Ana reach code beyond state baseline —
**verify with Building Safety at (714) 647-5800.**

## 2. C&D Waste Management Plan

Santa Ana uses a **C&D Waste Management Plan** (similar to Garden Grove's CDDMP).

### Requirements

- **Required for:** All new construction, additions > 500 sf, demolition.
- **Diversion goal:** ≥ 65% by weight of total C&D debris.
- **Submit with:** Building permit application.
- **Completion:** Submit hauler receipts after project completion.

### Tonnage Calculation (Residential New Construction)

| Project Type | Base Factor |
|-------------|-------------|
| New wood-frame residential | 4.0 lbs/sf |
| Stucco modifier (2-story) | × 1.04 |

> **NOTE:** The 4.0 lbs/sf factor source is unconfirmed (see `report/factual-sweep.md`).
> Do not cite on submitted forms without verifying the source. Contact the jurisdiction's
> CALGreen desk for their accepted calculation method.

## 3. Title 24 Part 6 — Energy Compliance

### Climate Zone

Santa Ana is in **CEC Climate Zone 8** (coastal OC / greater LA basin).
Same zone as Garden Grove — moderate heating/cooling loads.

### Compliance Path

- **Performance approach** via CF1R (EnergyPro or CBECC-Res) required for new construction.
- CF1R must be attached to plan check submittal.

### Mandatory Measures (key)

| Section | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| §150.0(a) | Insulation per Zone 8 prescriptive table |
| §150.0(c) | Fenestration U-factor + SHGC |
| §150.0(m)(1) | HVAC duct sealing and insulation |
| §150.0(n) | Water heating — heat pump or solar ready |
| §150.1(c)7 | EV charging — conduit rough-in for SFD |
| §150.1(c)14 | Solar-ready zone |

### HERS-Verified Measures (Zone 8 common)

- Central AC: duct leakage test required.
- Heat pump water heater: HERS verification if credited.
- Verified insulation (blown-in or spray foam).

HERS raters: CalCERTS (calcerts.com) or CHEERS (cheers.net).

## 4. Local Reach Code

As of 2024, Santa Ana had **not adopted a local energy reach code** beyond the state
2022 Part 6 baseline. Verify with Building Safety before submittal — a 2025 reach
code adoption is possible effective Jan 1, 2026.

## 5. Solar & Battery Storage

- Solar-ready zone required per §150.1(c)14 for all new SFD.
- PV permit: streamlined OTC per SB 379 for qualifying systems.
- Battery ESS: electrical permit; SAFD review for large systems (> 20 kWh).
