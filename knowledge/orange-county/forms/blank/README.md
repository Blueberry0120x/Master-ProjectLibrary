## Key Notes

- **unincorporated OC** means the county boundary area, not cities within OC.
- Many OC cities (Garden Grove, Santa Ana, Anaheim, etc.) have their own building departments.
- Water districts vary by area: Mesa Water, Irvine Ranch Water, El Toro Water, etc.
- **Special Inspection Program:** OCDS has a special inspector designation program — confirm requirements for engineered projects.
