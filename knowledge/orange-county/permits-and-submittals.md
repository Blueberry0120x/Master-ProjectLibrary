# Orange County — Permits & Submittals (Unincorporated)

**Authority:** OC Development Services (OCDS), a division of OC Public Works.
**Portal:** https://ocds.ocpublicworks.com
**Permit system:** **Accela Citizen Access (ACA)** — https://aca-prod.accela.com/OCCA/

---

## Permit Intake — Ministerial Building Permits

### Over-the-Counter (Express Review)

Available for simple work that meets prescriptive paths:
- Re-roof (like-for-like)
- Water heater, HVAC change-out
- Electrical service upgrade ≤ 200A (no panel relocation)
- Window change-out (same size, same opening)
- Minor interior remodel (no structural, no bearing walls)
- Pool / spa (on pre-approved plan)
- Signs (non-illuminated, in conformance)

**Submit:** walk-in at OCDS counter (601 N. Ross St. Santa Ana, 4th floor) OR
upload via ACA. Same-day issuance if complete.

### Standard Plan Check

Required for:
- New SFD / ADU / duplex
- Additions > 500 sf or with structural impact
- Commercial TI, new commercial, multifamily
- Pools in hillside/grading areas
- Any project requiring grading permit
- Any project in Coastal Zone or VHFHSZ

**Typical routing (parallel):**
1. Building (OCDS)
2. Planning/zoning (OCDS)
3. Grading / Hydrology (OC Public Works)
4. OCFA Planning & Development Services
5. OC Environmental Health (if OWTS, well, pool, food)
6. OC Sanitation District (if sewer connection)
7. Water agency (varies by service area — IRWD, MNWD, Serrano Water, etc.)
8. School district fees (per AB 2926)

**Processing time (typical):**
- 1st review: 4–6 weeks new SFD; 6–10 weeks commercial.
- Resubmittal: 2–4 weeks.
- Correction cycles: budget 2–3 rounds.

## Required Submittal — New Residential (SFD / ADU)

### Plan Sheets (min set)

| Sheet | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Title / Cover** | Project info, APN, code list, sheet index, site data, occupancy/construction type |
| **Site Plan** | Lot, setbacks, existing/proposed structures, dimensions, topo, utilities, driveway, grading extents, tree callouts |
| **Architectural — Floor Plans** | Dimensioned rooms, door/window schedules, egress, smoke/CO alarms |
| **Architectural — Elevations** | All 4 sides, heights, finish material, WUI compliance callouts if applicable |
| **Architectural — Sections** | Min 2 sections showing floor/ceiling assemblies |
| **Structural Plans** | Foundation, framing, shear, details |
| **Structural Calcs** | Stamped by CA-licensed engineer (gravity + lateral) |
| **T24 Energy** | CF1R-PRF-01 or CF1R-NCB (prescriptive); registered with CEC HERS provider |
| **CALGreen Checklist** | Mandatory measures, water/waste/IAQ |
| **Grading / Drainage Plan** | If > 50 cy cut/fill or triggering criteria; stamped civil |
| **SUSMP / WQMP** | If Priority Project (see `stormwater-wqmp.md`) |
| **ESCP / WPCP** | Construction-phase erosion control |
| **Soils Report** | Geotech report for foundation (new SFD always); expansive/liquefaction analysis |
| **Fire Sprinkler Plans** (NFPA 13D) | Deferred submittal to OCFA for new SFD/ADU |
| **Fuel Modification Plan** | If in/adjacent VHFHSZ or SRA — OCFA approval required |

### Application Forms

- Building Permit Application (ACA online)
- Owner-Builder Declaration or Contractor License
- Workers' Comp declaration
- Geotechnical Review Sheet (if soils report)
- School Fees form (paid at issuance)

## Discretionary Approvals

### Site Development Permit (SDP)

- Required for most new commercial, multifamily, non-exempt residential in some
  overlays.
- Reviewed by Zoning Administrator (ZA) at public hearing; 4–6 months typical.
- Findings: consistent with GP, compatible with area, adequate services,
  CEQA cleared.

### Use Permit (CUP)

- For conditionally permitted uses in base zone (e.g., daycare in R1, gas station
  in C1).
- ZA or Planning Commission.

### Variance

- Deviation from standards (setback, height, FAR).
- Must find: (a) special circumstances of property, (b) no special privilege,
  (c) hardship not self-imposed.

### Tentative Tract Map (5+ lots) / Tentative Parcel Map (≤ 4 lots)

- Subdivision Map Act, Gov. Code § 66410 et seq.
- Tract map to Planning Commission; Parcel map to Subdivision Committee.
- Final map recordation at OC Clerk-Recorder.

### Coastal Development Permit (CDP)

- Required in Coastal Zone per certified LCP.
- OC-administered for areas with certified LCP; otherwise Coastal Commission.
- Appeals to CCC for specified project types.

## Grading Permit (OC Public Works — Grading Manual)

Triggered by any of:
- Cut or fill volume > **50 cubic yards**
- Cut slope > 5 ft vertical, or fill > 3 ft
- Excavation > 5 ft deep
- Import/export of soil
- Grading in hillside areas (per adopted Hillside Management Area maps)
- Grading within a Coastal Zone, VHFHSZ, or floodplain

**Submittal:** Grading plan stamped by CA-licensed civil engineer, hydrology/
hydraulic report if drainage affected, soils report, ESCP.

**Bonds:** Performance bond + monumentation bond may be required; security per
OC Grading Manual.

**Inspection phases:** Pre-grading meeting → rough grade → fine grade → final.

---

## Encroachment Permit (OC Public Works)

- Required for any work in County-maintained ROW (driveway apron, sidewalk,
  curb cut, utility cut, traffic control).
- Insurance: $1M general liability (and $2M umbrella for major work); add
  County as additional insured.
- Permit via OC Public Works Construction Division.

---

## OWTS / Septic (OC Environmental Health)

- Required where no sewer (Silverado, Modjeska, Trabuco Cyn, parts of Coto,
  rural unincorporated).
- Percolation test by CA-registered civil engineer or REHS.
- Design per **OC Local Agency Management Program (LAMP)**, conforming to
  Statewide Water Board OWTS Policy (2012/amended).
- Setbacks: 100 ft from wells, 50 ft from property line, 10 ft from structure
  (typical; verify LAMP).
- Leachfield sized per soil loading rate × design flow.
- Separate permit; approval required before building permit issuance.

## Private Wells (OC Environmental Health)

- Permit required for new, replacement, or destruction of domestic wells.
- DWR Bulletin 74-81 / 74-90 construction standards.
- Setbacks: 100 ft from septic, 50 ft from sewer line.

---

## Pool / Spa Permits

- OCDS issues structural permit.
- OC Environmental Health reviews public pools (HOA, apartment, commercial)
  under CDPH / OC Pool Health Code.
- Private residential pool: building permit + barrier per CRC Appendix V /
  CBC 3109.

## Resubmittal Protocol

1. Download all corrections (OCDS, OCFA, OC Public Works, OC EH, water, sewer).
2. Prepare consolidated **Response to Corrections Matrix**: correction # → sheet
   # / section → response.
3. Revision-cloud all changes on plans with Δ1/Δ2 triangle markers.
4. Upload revised PDFs to ACA with revision number; response letter as cover.
5. Anticipate 2–4 week re-review.
