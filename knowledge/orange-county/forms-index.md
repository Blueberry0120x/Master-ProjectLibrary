## Notes

- **OC unincorporated is distinct from OC cities.** Garden Grove, Santa Ana, Anaheim,
  Irvine, etc. are incorporated cities with their own building departments.
  Use this file only for unincorporated OC parcels.
- **OCFA jurisdiction.** OCFA provides fire protection to most of unincorporated OC
  and many OC cities by contract. Verify OCFA jurisdiction vs. city fire dept.
- **WUI is significant in eastern/foothill OC.** Many Coto de Caza, Trabuco Canyon,
  and Las Flores parcels are in VHFHSZ. Chapter 7A and OCFA Fuel Modification Plan
  required.
