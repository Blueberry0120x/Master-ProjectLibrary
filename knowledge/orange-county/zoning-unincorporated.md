# Orange County — Zoning (Unincorporated Areas)

**Jurisdiction:** Unincorporated County of Orange only. Each of the 34 incorporated
cities has its own Zoning Code.

**Code location:** **Orange County Codified Ordinance, Title 7 — Planning and
Development, Division 7 — Zoning** (§ 7-9-1 et seq.).

**Administrator:** OC Development Services (OCDS) — Planning Division.

---

## Zoning Code Structure

- **Title 7 Div 7 Chapter 1** — General Provisions
- **Title 7 Div 7 Chapter 2** — Administrative Procedures (Use Permits, Variances,
  Site Development Permits, hearings)
- **Title 7 Div 7 Chapter 3** — Zoning Districts Established
- **Title 7 Div 7 Chapters 60–99** — District-by-district development standards
- **Title 7 Div 7 Chapter 150+** — Overlay zones, Specific Plans, Planned Communities

| Standard | R1 | E1 | E2 | E3 | E4 |

| Min lot area (sf) | 6,000 | 10,000 | 15,000–20,000 | 20,000–40,000 | 40,000+ |
| Min lot width (ft) | 60 | 70 | 80 | 100 | 100 |

| Rear setback (ft) | 25 (or 20 with garage) | 25 | 25 | 25 | 25 |

| Max lot coverage | 40% | 40% | 35% | 35% | 30% |

## Parking Standards (§ 7-9-145 et seq.)

| Use | Required Parking |
|-----|------------------|
| Single-family dwelling | 2 covered spaces (garage) per unit |
| Multifamily (studio) | 1 space / unit |
| Multifamily (1 br) | 1.5 spaces / unit |
| Multifamily (2+ br) | 2 spaces / unit + 0.5 guest per unit for 5+ units |
| Senior housing | 0.5–1 space / unit |
| Retail | 1 space / 250 sf GFA |
| Restaurant | 1 space / 100 sf GFA (1/75 if > 2,500 sf) |
| Office | 1 space / 250 sf GFA (1/300 for medical varies) |
| Industrial/warehouse | 1 space / 1,000 sf (warehouse); 1/500 (mfg) |
| Church / assembly | 1 space / 4 fixed seats |

**ADU parking:** 1 space per unit maximum; waived if within ½ mile of transit,
inside historic district, or on same lot as SFD with existing parking (per state
ADU law; see `adu-and-sb9.md`).

## Overlays and Special Areas

### Coastal Zone Overlay (§ 7-9-153 et seq.)

- Applies to unincorporated coastal areas (limited in OC — most coastline is
  incorporated: Laguna Beach, Newport Beach, Huntington Beach, Dana Point, San
  Clemente, Seal Beach).
- Coastal Development Permit (CDP) required in addition to base zoning permit.
- Certified Local Coastal Program (LCP) governs; appeals to CCC.

### Foothill / Trabuco Specific Plans (FTSP)

- Covers Foothill / Trabuco Canyon areas (Coto de Caza, Modjeska, Silverado,
  Trabuco Canyon, Cowan Heights).
- Overlays impose stricter fuel modification, grading, and rural-character
  standards on top of base E1–E4 zoning.
- Development must comply with both base zone AND Specific Plan text.

### North Tustin Specific Plan (NTSP)

- Lemon Heights / Cowan Heights / Lower Peters Canyon.
- Large-lot estate character; stricter height and FAR.

### Planned Communities (PC)

- Major PCs: Rancho Mission Viejo (RMV), Ladera Ranch, Coto de Caza, Rossmoor.
- Each has its own adopted PC text that supersedes base zoning.

### Scenic Highway Overlay

- Along designated scenic corridors (Ortega Hwy, PCH segments).
- Additional height/architectural review.

### Airport Environs Land Use Plan (AELUP)

- John Wayne Airport (SNA) and Fullerton Muni environs.
- Height limits, noise disclosure, CLUP consistency review by ALUC.

## Affordable Housing / Density

- **Density Bonus Law** (Gov. Code § 65915): 80% density bonus possible for
  100% affordable projects; state-controlled incentives.
- **Housing Element compliance:** OC unincorporated 6th Cycle Housing Element
  certified; includes Affordable Housing Overlay (AHO) sites.
- **AHO:** Allows ≥ 30 du/ac by right for qualifying affordable projects on
  designated inventory parcels.

## Discretionary Approvals (§ 7-9-150 Chapter 2 Administration)

| Action | Body | Threshold |
|--------|------|-----------|
| **Site Development Permit (SDP)** | Zoning Administrator or PC | Most new commercial/multifamily; some residential |
| **Use Permit (CUP)** | Zoning Administrator or PC | Conditionally permitted uses |
| **Variance** | Zoning Administrator or PC | Deviation from dev standards — must show hardship |
| **Tentative Tract Map** (5+ lots) | Planning Commission | Subdivision Map Act |
| **Tentative Parcel Map** (≤ 4 lots) | Subdivision Committee | — |
| **General Plan Amendment** | Board of Supervisors | — |
| **Zone Change** | Board of Supervisors | — |
| **Coastal Development Permit** | County or CCC | In Coastal Zone |

Appeals: Zoning Administrator → Planning Commission → Board of Supervisors.

## Accessory Structures

- Detached accessory structure (non-ADU): max 15 ft height, 120 sf without
  permit (per CRC), 5 ft from rear/side PL typical in R1.
- Pools/spas: 5 ft from PL (water's edge), barrier per CBC § 3109 / CRC AV.
- Fences: 6 ft rear/side, 3.5 ft front yard typical; see § 7-9-130.
