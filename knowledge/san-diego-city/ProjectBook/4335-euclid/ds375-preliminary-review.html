SECTION 3 — PROJECT SCOPE
Description of Proposed Project
Demolish existing 1,225 SF single-story commercial building (auto repair, built 1952, Group B) and construct a new 2-story mixed-use development on a 6,250 SF lot in CUPD-CU-2-4 zone. Proposed program: 9 residential units (Group R-2, ~5,760 SF net residential) + 1 ground-floor commercial space (Group B, ~640 SF) + common lobby and utility areas. Total GBA ~6,845 SF. FAR 1.10 of 2.0 permitted. Four attached wood-frame (VB) buildings at 0-ft side setbacks. NFPA 13 automatic sprinkler system throughout. Stucco exterior. Site within Transit Priority Area (TPA) — 0 auto parking required per AB 2097. Public improvements proposed on Euclid Ave frontage. SB 330 Preliminary Application attached (Section 9).
