## 2. Stormwater Requirements

### Project Tier

| Condition | Tier |
|-----------|------|
| New construction creating ≥ 10,000 sf impervious | Priority Development Project (PDP) — BMP/SWQMP required |
| New construction < 10,000 sf | Standard Project — basic BMPs |
| SFD additions / ADU | Standard Project (typically) |

### Standard Project BMPs

1. **Site Design BMPs** — minimize impervious, direct runoff to landscape.
2. **Source Control BMPs** — trash enclosure, storage away from drains.
3. **Construction BMPs** — erosion/sediment control, concrete washout.

Submit a **BMP Site Plan** showing BMP locations.

### Priority Development Project (PDP)

- **Storm Water Quality Management Plan (SWQMP)** or **BMP Design Manual** approach.
- Low Impact Development (LID) treatment controls: bioretention, infiltration, filter strips.
- Hydromodification analysis — pre/post peak runoff comparison.
- O&M Agreement recorded against property.

## 3. Grading Permits

| Condition | Permit Required |
|-----------|----------------|
| Cut or fill > 50 cubic yards | Grading permit |
| Creating slopes > 3:1 | Grading permit |
| Hillside development (slopes ≥ 15% avg) | Grading permit + Hillside Development Permit |
| Grading in FEMA floodplain | Grading permit + Floodplain Development Permit |
| Grading in or near watercourse | Coordinated with Engineering |

### Grading Plan Requirements (EMC Title 29)

- Prepared by licensed Civil Engineer (CA PE).
- Existing and proposed grade contours at 1-ft intervals on steep terrain.
- Cut/fill quantities, haul routes, disposal site.
- Slope stability analysis (geotechnical report) if cuts > 5 ft or fills > 3 ft.
- Erosion and sediment control plan.
- Drainage study for projects with significant runoff changes.
- Revegetation plan for all disturbed slopes.

## 4. Flood Zone Compliance

Escondido has FEMA-mapped floodplains along Escondido Creek, Ysidora Basin,
and other drainage corridors.

- Check FEMA MSC for specific parcel: https://msc.fema.gov/portal/home
- Zone AE parcels: finished floor at BFE + 1 ft freeboard.
- No fill in floodway without compensatory storage analysis.
- Floodplain Development Permit from Engineering required.
- LOMA available if site data shows parcel is above BFE.

---

## 5. Hillside Drainage Design

For hillside lots (Harmony Grove, Kit Carson Park area, Lake Wohlford vicinity):

- Drainage must be designed to prevent erosion on manufactured slopes.
- Retaining wall drainage: weep holes and granular backfill per standard details.
- Outlet protection: rock energy dissipators at downslope outlets.
- No concentrated discharge onto adjacent properties.

---

## 6. Construction General Permit (≥ 1 acre)

Land disturbance ≥ 1 acre requires SWRCB CGP:
- File NOI in SMARTS before grading.
- Prepare and implement SWPPP.
- File NOT after final stabilization.
