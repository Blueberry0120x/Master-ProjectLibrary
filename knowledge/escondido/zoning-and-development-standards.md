## 2. Residential Development Standards (Single-Family)

### Setbacks — R-1 Zone (most common SFD zone)

| Setback | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| Front yard | 20 ft |
| Interior side yard | 5 ft |
| Street side yard (corner lot) | 10 ft |
| Rear yard | 15 ft (or 20% of lot depth, whichever greater) |

**Verify exact setbacks for your zone in EMC Title 33 — hillside overlays and
specific development areas modify these standards.**

### Building Height

| Zone | Maximum Height |
|------|---------------|
| RE, R-A | 35 ft |
| R-1, R-1-7 | 35 ft; 2 stories |
| R-2 | 35 ft |
| R-3 | 35 ft |
| R-4 | 45 ft |

### Lot Coverage

| Zone | Max Coverage |
|------|-------------|
| RE, R-A | 25% |
| R-1 | 40% |
| R-1-7 | 45–50% |
| R-2, R-3 | 50% |
| R-4 | 60% |

### Floor Area Ratio

EMC Title 33 may apply FAR in some zones or overlay areas.
For standard R-1 residential, lot coverage and height are the primary controls.
Verify current FAR applicability with Planning at (760) 839-4671.

## 3. Hillside Development Overlay

Escondido's Hillside Development Ordinance (EMC Title 33) applies to parcels
with average natural slopes exceeding a specified threshold.

### Triggers

| Condition | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Average natural slope ≥ 15% | Hillside Development Permit may be required |
| Slopes ≥ 25% | Development severely restricted; geotechnical report required |
| Slopes ≥ 40% | Generally undevelopable; open space preservation expected |

### Hillside Requirements

- Grading minimized — follow natural contours where possible.
- Cut/fill balanced on-site; excess export requires hauler permit.
- Retaining walls: max exposed height per zone; terracing preferred.
- Slope stability analysis by licensed geotechnical engineer.
- Revegetation plan for all disturbed slopes.
- Fire-safe landscaping (VHFHSZ parcels).

## 4. Parking Requirements

### Residential

| Use | Required |
|-----|----------|
| SFD (1–3 bedrooms) | 2 spaces (1 covered) |
| SFD (4+ bedrooms) | 3 spaces |
| ADU ≤ 500 sf or studio/1BR | 0 (state law) |
| ADU > 500 sf | 1 space |
| R-2 duplex | 2 spaces per unit |
| R-3 / R-4 | 1.5–2 spaces/unit + 0.25 guest |

State ADU law overrides local minimums within ½ mile of transit.

### Garage Dimensions

- Minimum clear interior: 20 ft × 20 ft (2-car); 10 ft × 20 ft (1-car).

## 5. Special Overlay Zones

| Overlay | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Specific Planning Area (SPA) | Master-planned communities (Harmony Grove, Escondido Ranch) — separate standards |
| Downtown Escondido Overlay | Enhanced design review, reduced parking, pedestrian focus |
| Historic Overlay | Properties in historic survey area — design compatibility review |
| Agricultural Preserve (AP) | Eastern parcels; limits non-ag development; LAFCO review |
| Flood Plain Overlay (FP) | FEMA SFHA parcels — BFE, freeboard, floodplain permit |
| Airport Influence Area (AIA) | Palomar Airport (McClellan-Palomar) FAA Part 77 surfaces |

---

## 6. Density Bonus (State Law)

Escondido must grant density bonuses per Gov Code 65915:
- 15% LI or 8% VLI → ~22.5% bonus
- 24% VLI → 50% bonus
- 100% affordable → 80% bonus
