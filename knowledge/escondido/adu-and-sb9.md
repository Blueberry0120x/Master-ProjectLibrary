# Escondido — ADU & SB 9

Source: California Gov Code §65852.2 + EMC Title 33.
State ADU law governs when local standards are more restrictive.

---

## 1. State ADU Law Floor

See `../_shared/statewide-adu-law.md` for full state-law coverage.
Key points: ministerial approval, 65-day deadline, 4 ft setbacks for new detached ADUs,
no parking within ½ mile of transit.

---

## 2. Escondido Local ADU Standards

### Permitted Zones

- R-1, R-1-7, R-2: one ADU + one JADU per lot with existing or proposed SFD.
- R-A, RE: ADU permitted per state law; check EMC for local size or design conditions.
- R-3, R-4: multifamily bonus ADU rules apply.

### ADU Size Limits

| Type | Maximum Size |
|------|-------------|
| JADU | 500 sf (within existing structure) |
| Attached ADU | 50% of primary dwelling sf, max 1,200 sf |
| Detached ADU | 1,200 sf |

### Setbacks

| Type | Setback |
|------|---------|
| New detached ADU — rear | 4 ft minimum (state law) |
| New detached ADU — side | 4 ft minimum (state law) |
| Conversion of existing structure | May retain nonconforming setback |

### Height

- Detached ADU within 4 ft of rear/side: max 16 ft.
- ADU over garage: up to 24 ft if base zone allows.

### Hillside ADUs

ADUs on hillside lots (average natural slope ≥ 15%) must comply with:
- EMC Title 33 Hillside Development requirements.
- Grading for ADU access and foundation must comply with grading limits.
- Additional geotechnical review may be required.
- State law protects the ADU from outright denial due to hillside standards —
  but objective hillside standards still apply. Contact Planning.

## 3. Fire Sprinklers

EFD requires NFPA 13D sprinklers for **new SFD** construction. For ADUs:
- **Attached ADU:** typically triggers sprinkler requirement if primary requires them.
- **Detached ADU:** confirm trigger with EFD at (760) 839-4600.
- **WUI parcels:** additional defensible space and Chapter 7A materials required.

---

## 4. SB 9 (Two-Unit / Urban Lot Split)

Gov Code §§65852.21 and 66411.7.

### Two-Unit Development

- 2 primary units on any SFD lot in urbanized area (most of Escondido qualifies).
- Ministerial; 4 ft rear/side setbacks.
- **WUI exclusion:** Parcels in VHFHSZ may be excluded from SB 9 two-unit development.
  Verify on CAL FIRE FHSZ map.
- Hillside parcels: objective hillside standards apply even under ministerial SB 9.

### Urban Lot Split

- Minimum post-split: 1,200 sf each.
- Tentative Parcel Map required.
- Owner-occupancy 3 years (lot split only).

---

## 5. Common Issues in Escondido

- **WUI exclusions.** Many Escondido parcels are in VHFHSZ. WUI exclusion from SB 9
  is real — always check before advising client on development potential.
- **Hillside grading for ADU.** Pad grading for detached ADU on steep lot can
  trigger a full hillside development permit and soils report.
- **School fees.** Both EUSD and EUHSD levy Level 1 fees for new residential > 500 sf.
  Two separate fee certifications required before permit issuance.
- **Vallecitos Water District.** Water/sewer capacity fees paid to Vallecitos, not
  the City. Will-serve letter from Vallecitos required at permit application.
