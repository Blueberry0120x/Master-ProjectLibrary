## 2. C&D Waste Management Plan

- **Required for:** All new construction, additions > 500 sf, demolition.
- **Diversion goal:** ≥ 65% by weight.
- **Submit with:** Building permit application.
- **Completion form:** Submit with hauler receipts after project.

> **NOTE:** 4.0 lbs/sf debris factor source is unconfirmed. Do not cite on

## 3. Title 24 Part 6 — Energy Compliance

### Climate Zone

Escondido is in **CEC Climate Zone 7** (inland San Diego County — warmer than coastal).

Key Zone 7 characteristics:
- Higher cooling loads than coastal zones.
- Higher ceiling insulation requirements.
- Lower SHGC limits on fenestration than Zone 8.

**Zone 7 is more demanding than Zone 8** — verify your CF1R is generated for Zone 7,
not a coastal zone, when working in Escondido.

### Compliance Path

- Performance approach via CF1R required for new SFD/ADU.
- CF1R attached to plan check submittal.

### Mandatory Measures (key Zone 7)

| Section | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| §150.0(a) | Insulation — higher R-values than Zone 8 for ceilings |
| §150.0(c) | Fenestration U-factor + SHGC (more restrictive than Zone 8) |
| §150.0(m)(1) | HVAC duct sealing and insulation |
| §150.0(n) | Water heating — heat pump preferred |
| §150.1(c)7 | EV charging conduit rough-in |
| §150.1(c)14 | Solar-ready zone |

### HERS-Verified Measures (Zone 7)

- Central AC duct leakage test common.
- Verified attic insulation (blown-in).
- Heat pump water heater verification if credited.

## 4. Local Reach Code

As of 2024, Escondido had not confirmed adoption of a local energy reach code.
Verify with Building Division before submittal — the 2025 state code cycle may
prompt local adoption changes.

---

## 5. Solar & Battery Storage

- Solar-ready zone required for all new SFD per §150.1(c)14.
- PV: OTC per SB 379.
- Battery ESS: electrical permit; EFD review for > 20 kWh.
