## Notes

- **Climate Zone 7** — all energy calculations must use Zone 7, not Zone 8. Inland
  Escondido is significantly warmer than coastal SD cities.
- **Two school districts** — both EUSD and EUHSD levies required. Budget time to
  get both certifications before permit issuance.
- **Vallecitos will-serve** — get this letter early; Vallecitos can take 2–3 weeks.
- **WUI parcels** — Chapter 7A materials list must be on the plans before plan check.
  EFD will flag missing WUI callouts as a correction.
