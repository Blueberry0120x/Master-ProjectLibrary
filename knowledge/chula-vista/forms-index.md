## Energy / Title 24

| Form | Name | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| CF1R | Certificate of Compliance — Residential | CEC-approved software; Climate Zone 7 or 10 (verify parcel) |
| CF2R | Certificate of Installation | Contractor signed |
| CF3R | Certificate of Verification | HERS rater signed |

**Note on Climate Zone:** Chula Vista spans CEC Climate Zones 7 (inland) and 10
(some western/coastal portions). Confirm zone for your specific address before
running compliance calculations.

## CAP Reach Code

Chula Vista has adopted a **Climate Action Plan (CAP) reach code** requiring
all-electric new residential construction (no new natural gas service for SFD
effective 2020 update). Verify current CAP requirements with Building Division.

## Notes

- **CAP all-electric requirement** — Chula Vista prohibits new natural gas service
  in new SFD construction per the CAP reach code. Heat pump HVAC and heat pump
  water heater are standard design for new SFD/ADU.
- **Climate Zone:** Inland CV parcels use Zone 7; verify before running CF1R.
- **Two school districts** — both CVESD and SUHSD certifications required.
- **No WMF** — CV uses a C&D Waste Management Plan, not SD City's WMF.
