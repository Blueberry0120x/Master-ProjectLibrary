## CALGreen / C&D Waste

| Form | Name | Source | Required When |
|------|------|--------|---------------|
| CALGreen Residential Checklist | Mandatory measures (Part 11 Ch 4) | Contact DSD — not separately indexed | Attach to all new residential |
| C&D Waste Management Report (WMR) | ≥65% diversion; administered by Environmental Services | Contact Env. Services (619) 585-5669 or chulavistaca.gov/departments/clean/environmental-services/construction-demolition-debris | All new construction >1,000 SF |
| C&D Completion Form | Post-project hauler receipts | Same as above | At project close |

> **Note:** Chula Vista uses "WMR" (Waste Management Report), NOT San Diego's "WMF" (Waste Management Form). Different form, different submittal process.

## CAP All-Electric Requirement

> ⚠ Chula Vista prohibits new natural gas service in new SFD construction per the **CAP reach code** (Climate Action Plan). All new SFD/ADU must be **all-electric**: heat pump HVAC + heat pump water heater. No new gas service allowed.
