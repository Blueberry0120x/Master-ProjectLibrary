# Chula Vista — Zoning & Development Standards (CVMC Title 19)

Chula Vista Municipal Code **Title 19 (Planning and Zoning)** governs zones, development
standards, and use regulations citywide. Planned Communities (PC zone) have their own
**Sectional Planning Area (SPA) Plans / PC District Regulations** that supersede base
zoning — verify the controlling SPA for eastern properties.

## Residential Development Standards (CVMC 19.24 – 19.28, typical)

| Standard | R-E | R-1 | R-1-5 | R-2 | R-3 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Min lot size | 20,000 sf | 7,000 sf | 5,000 sf | 7,000 sf | per density |
| Min lot width | 100 ft | 60 ft | 50 ft | 60 ft | 60 ft |
| Front setback | 25 ft | 15 ft (main)* | 15 ft | 15 ft | 15 ft |
| Side setback (interior) | 10 ft | 5 ft / 10 ft combined | 5 ft | 5 ft | 10 ft |
| Side setback (street/corner) | 15 ft | 10 ft | 10 ft | 10 ft | 10 ft |
| Rear setback | 25 ft | 20 ft | 15 ft | 20 ft | 15 ft |
| Max height | 28 ft / 2.5 stories | 28 ft / 2.5 stories | 28 ft | 28 ft | 45 ft |
| Max lot coverage | 40% | 40% | 50% | 50% | 50% |

\* Garage setback typically 20 ft in R-1 to accommodate driveway parking.

**FAR:** Base R-1 FAR **0.45–0.55** (varies by lot size; large-lot reduction applies).
Verify current GP/zoning tables. PC zones set FAR per SPA.

## Urban Core Specific Plan (UCSP)

Third Avenue / downtown. **Form-based** standards with frontage types, build-to lines,
active-ground-floor requirements, and step-back massing. Districts: Urban Core Transit
Focus, Village, Gateway, Corridor. Heights to **84 ft** in Urban Core Transit (plus
bonus for affordable). Parking reductions in TOD zone.

## Bayfront Specific Plan (CVBMP)

Chula Vista Bayfront Master Plan — joint Port of San Diego / City. Districts: Sweetwater,
Harbor, Otay. Includes Resort (Gaylord Pacific), Commercial Recreation, Residential Mixed
Use, Parks, and Ecological Buffer. Coastal Commission overlay applies.

## Parking Standards (CVMC 19.62)

| Use | Minimum Parking |
|---|---|
| SFD | 2 covered (garage) |
| SFD 5+ bedrooms | 3 spaces (1 covered) |
| ADU | 1 space (waived if within ½ mi transit per state law) |
| Duplex | 2 per unit (1 covered) |
| Multifamily studio / 1 BR | 1.5 spaces |
| Multifamily 2 BR | 2.0 spaces |
| Multifamily 3+ BR | 2.5 spaces |
| Guest parking | 1 per 4 units (multifamily) |
| Retail | 1 per 200 sf |
| Office | 1 per 300 sf |
| Restaurant | 1 per 2.5 seats or 1 per 100 sf |
| Industrial | 1 per 1,000 sf |

**AB 2097** — no parking minimums within ½ mile of major transit stop (state overrides).

## Landscape (CVMC Title 20 / Chapter 19.58; Landscape Manual)

- **City of Chula Vista Landscape Manual** governs submittal requirements
- **Water Conservation** per CCR Title 23 MWELO — projects >500 sf landscape area
- Non-residential / multifamily → Design Review includes landscape plan
- **Brush management** in VHFHSZ — see fire-cvfd.md
- Front yard landscape required (max % hardscape / turf limits)
- Irrigation controller: smart / ET-based required per MWELO

## Density Bonus / Affordable

- State Density Bonus Law (Gov Code 65915) applies — up to 80% bonus for 100% affordable
- Chula Vista Balanced Communities / Inclusionary Housing — 10% affordable requirement for
  subdivisions ≥50 units (5% low + 5% moderate) per Housing Element

## Hillside Development

- Chapter 19.22 (Hillside Modifying District)
- Slopes ≥25% — restricted development, slope-density formula
- Slopes 10–25% — hillside design standards apply
- Grading minimization, natural slope preservation
