## School Fees

Multiple school districts serve San Diego County unincorporated areas.
The applicable district depends on parcel location.

| District (common) | Contact |
|-------------------|---------|
| Grossmont Union High School District | (619) 644-8000 |
| Cajon Valley Union School District | (619) 588-3000 |
| San Dieguito Union High School District | (760) 753-6491 |
| Ramona Unified School District | (760) 787-2000 |

**Always verify the applicable district with PDS at submittal** — school district
boundaries do not follow city/county political boundaries.

## Notes

- **WUI is widespread in unincorporated SD County.** Assume VHFHSZ unless
  confirmed otherwise — Chapter 7A materials and defensible space are the default
  for new construction in most rural/hillside areas.
- **No WMF.** County uses a C&D Waste Management Plan, not San Diego City's WMF.
- **Multiple school districts.** Two certifications (elementary + high school)
  are often required — same as Escondido.
