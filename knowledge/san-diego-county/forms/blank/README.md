## Key Notes for Unincorporated County

- **WUI (VHFHSZ):** Much of unincorporated SD County is in VHFHSZ. Chapter 7A (CBC) ignition-resistant construction required.
- **Septic vs. Sewer:** Many rural parcels have septic systems; confirm sewer availability with PDS early.
- **SDCFA Fire:** San Diego County Fire Authority (SDCFA) reviews fire plans for most unincorporated areas. (619) 956-4800.
- **Water Districts:** Valley Center, Ramona, Padre Dam, Otay Water, Helix Water, etc. — confirm by parcel.
