# Permits & Submittals — County of San Diego (Unincorporated)

**Authority:** Planning & Development Services (PDS); Department of Environmental Health & Quality (DEHQ); Department of Public Works (DPW); County Fire Authority or local FPD.
**Portal:** Accela Citizen Access — https://publicservices.sandiegocounty.gov/CitizenAccess/
**Counter:** 5510 Overland Ave, Ste 110, San Diego CA 92123. (858) 565-5920.

> **Differs from City of San Diego:** County runs a single Accela instance separate from City's OpenDSD. Fees, review durations, and routing differ. Electrical utility and water districts are multiple vendors (SDG&E is universal; water can be San Diego County Water Authority wholesale, various special districts, or private wells).

---

## 1. Two Tracks: Discretionary vs. Ministerial

| Track | Description | CEQA? | Hearing? |
|-------|-------------|-------|----------|
| **Discretionary** | Requires judgment/public notice (MUP, variance, map, rezone, GPA) | Yes — CE, MND, or EIR | Usually |
| **Ministerial** | Staff checks compliance with adopted standards (building permit, ADU, OTC programs) | Statutorily exempt | No |

### Common Discretionary Permits (§7000 series)

| Permit | Trigger | Decision-maker |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| **Major Use Permit (MUP)** | Large-scale CUPs (commercial in ag zone, large mining, cannabis) | Planning Commission |
| **Minor Use Permit (mUP)** | Smaller CUPs (home occupation exceeding by-right, small ag processing) | PDS Director (admin hearing) |
| **Site Plan Permit** | Required by overlay/zone (Design Review, Hillside, BRC, Scenic) | PDS Director or PC |
| **Administrative Permit** | Minor deviations (fence height, setback encroachments) | PDS Director |
| **Variance** | Hardship relief from strict zoning standard (Gov Code §65906) | PC or BOS |
| **Tentative Map (TM)** | 5+ lot subdivision | PC then BOS |
| **Parcel Map (TPM)** | 2–4 lot split | PDS Director |
| **Specific Plan / Specific Plan Amendment (SPA)** | Master-planned community (e.g., Valiano, Harmony Grove Village) | BOS |
| **General Plan Amendment (GPA)** | Change land use designation | BOS |
| **Rezone (R)** | Change zoning designator | BOS |
| **Boundary Adjustment / Certificate of Compliance** | Minor lot-line relocation | PDS Director |

### Hearing/Approval Bodies

1. **PDS Zoning Administrator / Director** — admin decisions.
2. **Planning Commission (PC)** — 7 members; appeals from ZA, major discretionary.
3. **Board of Supervisors (BOS)** — 5 members; legislative actions + final appeals.
4. **Administrative Hearing Officer (AHO)** — minor use permits, admin variances.

## 2. CEQA Compliance

Every discretionary permit is a **"project"** under CEQA unless an exemption applies.

| CEQA Finding | Typical Trigger |
|--------------|-----------------|
| **Statutory Exemption** | Ministerial permits, emergency, feasibility studies |
| **Categorical Exemption (CE)** | Class 1 existing facilities, Class 3 new small structures (up to 10,000 SF non-res on serviced parcels, 3 SFDs in urban area), Class 32 infill (urban, ≤5 ac, adequate services) |
| **Negative Declaration (ND)** | No significant effects identified |
| **Mitigated Negative Declaration (MND)** | Effects reduced to less-than-significant via mitigation |
| **Environmental Impact Report (EIR)** | Potentially significant unmitigable effects |

**County CEQA Guidelines** adopted 2013, updated periodically. Thresholds document sets numeric thresholds for VMT (CEQA-SB 743), air quality, biology (MSCP/PAMA), GHG, noise, hydrology.

## 3. Ministerial Building Permit Process

### Required Submittals for a New SFD

1. **Application & site plan** (Accela intake)
2. **Site plan sheet** showing parcel with dimensions, setbacks, zoning designators, existing/proposed structures, driveway, well/septic locations if applicable, easements, contours 2-ft interval
3. **Floor plans** (each level, dimensioned, use labels, area tabulation)
4. **Elevations** (all 4 with natural grade + finish grade, heights, materials)
5. **Building sections & details**
6. **Structural** (foundation, framing, lateral — stamped engineer for 2-story or complex lateral per CRC R301)
7. **Title 24 Part 6 Energy (CF1R compliance form + calculations)**
8. **CALGreen Part 11 checklist (mandatory measures)**
9. **Grading / drainage plan** — even if below 200 CY, must show positive drainage and erosion control
10. **Fire access / defensible space plan** — access driveway, address, Zone 0/1/2, water supply
11. **OWTS (Septic) Layout** (DEHQ pre-approval) IF no sewer — leach field, reserve, well buffer ≥100 ft
12. **Well permit** (DEHQ) if no community water
13. **School fees receipt** (state-mandated, paid to local school district)
14. **RTCIP** (SANDAG Regional Traffic Congestion Improvement Program) fee
15. **TIF** (County Traffic Impact Fee) per TIF Zone
16. **PLDO** (Park Land Dedication Ordinance) fee per CPA
17. **Fire district fee / plan review** (SDCF or local FPD)
18. **Driveway encroachment permit** (DPW) if onto County-maintained road

### Reviews Routed

| Division | Looks for |
|----------|-----------|
| PDS Zoning | Setbacks, height, coverage, use, overlays |
| PDS Building | CBC/CRC/CEC/CMC/CPC/CALGreen/CEnC |
| DPW Land Dev Eng | Grading, drainage, roads, TIF |
| DEH Land & Water Quality | OWTS, well, hazmat |
| Fire (SDCF or FPD) | WUI, defensible space, access, water supply |
| School District | Fee calc (external) |
| Utility (SDG&E/water dist) | Service verification |

### Review Timelines (typical, not guaranteed)

- **1st review:** 4–8 weeks
- **Each re-submittal:** 2–4 weeks
- **Permit expiration:** Application expires 12 months from filing without activity; issued permit expires 12 months from issuance or per last inspection (CBC §105.5).

## 4. Over-the-Counter (OTC) and Express Programs

| Program | Scope |
|---------|-------|
| **Express Plan Check** | Small projects (≤500 SF addition, re-roofs, water heaters, HVAC change-outs, solar PV, pools in certain zones) — same-day or next-day |
| **Owner-Builder** | SFD on own parcel, sign CSLB owner-builder affidavit — same permit path, different contractor bond requirements |
| **PV Solar/Energy Storage Express** | Statewide SB 379 / AB 379 — automated online solar app (SolarAPP+) for qualifying PV/ESS |
| **ADU Express** | See adu-and-sb9.md |
| **Emergency / disaster rebuild** | Waived fees for presidentially-declared disasters (post-fire reconstruction programs) |

## 5. Department of Environmental Health — Wells & OWTS

### Wells
- Permit required before drilling: **well permit** + **construction inspection**.
- Setback: **100 ft from leach field**, 50 ft from septic tank, 50 ft from property line (typical), 100 ft from corral.
- Water-quality testing (bacteria, nitrate) pre-occupancy.

### On-Site Wastewater Treatment Systems (OWTS / Septic)
County is designated **Local Agency Management Program (LAMP)** under State Water Resources Control Board OWTS Policy. Three **tiers**:

| Tier | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Tier 1** | Standard conventional gravity system on approved soils/percolation |
| **Tier 2** | Supplemental treatment (ATU, sand filter) — smaller lots, poor soils, high groundwater |
| **Tier 3** | Engineered / advanced (press. distribution, drip, mounds) — most complex sites |

**Design requirements:**
- **Percolation test** by licensed professional (soils engineer / REHS-qualified), 3 test holes minimum (2 primary + 1 reserve area).
- **100% Reserve area** required (future replacement leach field). Reserve must meet all setbacks independently.
- **Setbacks (typical — verify LAMP):**
  - Leach field to well: 100 ft
  - Leach field to property line: 5–10 ft
  - Leach field to cut slope: 4× slope height (typically 25–50 ft)
  - Leach field to creek/drainage: 100 ft (200 ft if near sensitive water body)
  - Septic tank to dwelling: 5 ft
  - Leach field to dwelling: 8 ft
- **Min lot size** for new OWTS depends on soil perc rate and water source; practically 0.5–1 ac minimum.

## 6. Road Access, Easements, Driveway

- **Public frontage:** encroachment permit (DPW) for driveway onto County-maintained road.
- **Private road access** must meet fire access standards (see fire-and-wildfire.md). Recorded ingress/egress easement required if crossing another parcel.
- **Road Maintenance Agreement (RMA):** recorded against all parcels sharing private road ≥3 users (per DPW private road standards, also insurer/lender demand).
- **Dedication:** may be required (deeded ROW to County) when building permit triggers General Plan Mobility Element road alignment.

## 7. Fees (Indicative — always verify current fee schedule)

| Fee | Basis |
|-----|-------|
| **Building Plan Check** | Valuation-based per PDS fee schedule |
| **Building Permit** | Valuation × factor |
| **PLDO** (Park Land Dedication Ordinance) | Per CPA, per dwelling — ranges ~$4,000–$15,000/DU |
| **TIF** (Traffic Impact Fee) | Per County TIF Zone — ~$3,000–$12,000/DU |
| **RTCIP** (SANDAG Regional) | Statutorily adjusted annually — ~$2,800–$3,500/DU (2025 figures) |
| **School fees** | Level 1 statewide $4.79/SF residential (2025); adjusted biennially |
| **Fire District fee / plan check** | Varies — SDCF typically $500–$1,500 plan review |
| **DEH OWTS plan review** | ~$1,500–$3,500 |
| **DEH Well permit** | ~$800 |
| **CA Strong Motion + Green Building surcharges** | Statewide adders |
