# WMF Procedural Knowledge Base
# City of San Diego - Waste Management Form

## When to Submit

- WMF is submitted AT PERMIT APPLICATION, not after approval.
- Required attachment for DSD to process your permit.
- Submitting before PMT is issued is correct and expected.

## Permit Number Field

- Form asks for PMT-XXXXXXX number (omit "PMT-" prefix).
- If PMT not yet issued: leave blank OR enter PRJ number with a note.
- DSD staff will assign PMT at permit issuance.

## C&D Recycling Deposit

- Paid at PERMIT ISSUANCE (not at application).
- Amount based on project valuation, typically $50-75 per $1,000 valuation.
- For a 1,200 SF ADU at ~$300K valuation: expect ~$1,500-3,000.
- FULLY REFUNDABLE if weight receipts submitted within 180 days of final inspection.
- Contact DSD at (619) 446-5000 for exact deposit amount.

## CALGreen Diversion Requirement

- 65% minimum for permits issued after July 1, 2016.
- Formula: (Total A / Total C) x 100 = Recycling %
- Must use certified C&D recycling facilities.
- Must use franchised haulers (recommended).

## Certified Facilities - San Diego

- Miramar Greenery: 8108 Miramar Rd, San Diego, CA 92126 (recycling)
- Miramar Landfill: 5180 Convoy St, San Diego, CA 92111 (residual trash only)
- Full list: sandiego.gov/environmental-services/recycling/cnd

## Franchised Haulers

- EDCO: Serves ZIP 92841 (7201 Garden Grove Blvd, STE A area)
- Full list: sandiego.gov/environmental-services/recycling/cnd/haulers

## Post-Construction Submittal

- Collect weight receipts from hauler/facility for EVERY load.
- Submit all receipts to ESD within 180 days of final inspection.
- Submit to: Environmental Services Dept, San Diego
- Email: [email protected]
- Without receipts: deposit is forfeited.

## One Form Per Permit Rule

- Do NOT list multiple permits on one WMF.
- If project has multiple permits (e.g., grading + building), file separate WMFs.

## CALGreen Base Estimation Factors (CalRecycle)

- New residential wood-frame: 4.0 lbs/SF
- 2-story + stucco modifier: x1.04
- Typical material distribution for new 2-story stucco ADU:
  - Wood & Pallets: 22% of total (largest)
  - Stucco: 16%
  - Drywall: 16%
  - Garbage: 8%
  - Mixed C&D: 8%
  - Roofing: 8%
  - Dirt: 8%
  - Cardboard: 6%
  - Scrap Metal: 4%
  - Landscape: 4%
