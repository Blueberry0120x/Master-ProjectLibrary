## Zoning — Home in Tacoma Reform

Tacoma is actively rezoning all residential parcels. Legacy R-1/R-2 zones are being
replaced with **Urban Residential (UR-1, UR-2, UR-3)**. Always verify current zone at
[dart.cityoftacoma.org](https://dart.cityoftacoma.org/) before using setback or density figures.

Legacy R-1 standards (may no longer apply):
- Front setback: 25 ft
- Max height: 35 ft
- FAR: N/A for residential
