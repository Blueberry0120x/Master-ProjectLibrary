## Typical Plan Contents (across most sources)

- Floor plan (dimensioned)
- Four elevations (front, rear, left, right)
- Wall framing plan
- Roof framing plan / rafter details
- Skid or concrete foundation detail
- Materials and cut list
- Step-by-step assembly instructions

## Permit Submittal Notes (California)

For a residential storage shed permit in CA cities (e.g., Carlsbad, San Diego):

- Plans typically need: floor plan + all 4 elevations + framing details
- Add a **site plan** showing the shed location, property lines, and setback dimensions
- Confirm local setback minimums (typically 5 ft rear/side for detached accessory structures)
- Sheds ≤ 120 sq ft may be **exempt from permit** in many CA jurisdictions — verify locally
- For permit-required sheds: structural calcs may be required depending on size and jurisdiction
- Carlsbad standalone plumbing permit (if adding sink/hose bib): flat $166 fee
