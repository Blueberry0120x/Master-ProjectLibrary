# Carlsbad Permit Package — Residential Remodel + Plumbing
**Scope:** Bathroom addition, full kitchen remodel, full bath remodel, re-pipe, new fixtures
**City:** Carlsbad, CA | Building Division: 1635 Faraday Ave, Carlsbad CA 92008

---

## Step 1 — Determine Valuation First

Valuation = materials + labor for ALL trades (plumbing, electrical, mechanical, framing, finishes).

| Threshold | Consequence |
|---|---|
| Any amount | B-1 + B-5 + 3 sets of plans required |
| ≥ $60,000 | B-50 CAP form + CF1-R energy doc + EV charging infrastructure required |

For this scope (bathroom addition + full kitchen + full bath + re-pipe), expect valuation **well above $60k**.

---

## Step 2 — Forms Checklist

- [ ] **B-1** — Residential Building Permit Application *(signed + dated)*
- [ ] **B-5** — Residential Construction Submittal Checklist
- [ ] **B-50** — Climate Action Plan Consistency Checklist *(if valuation ≥ $60k — likely required)*
- [ ] **B-61** — Owner-Builder Acknowledgement *(only if owner doing the work, no licensed contractor)*
- [ ] **CF1-R** — Energy Compliance Document *(required with B-50; must be imprinted on plans)*

---

## Step 3 — Construction Plans (3 sets required, PDF 24"×36" landscape)

All plans must be **signed by plan preparer** before submittal.

### Architectural / General
- [ ] Floor plan — dimensioned, room labels, all window/door sizes and locations
- [ ] Existing vs. proposed floor plan (show what's changing)
- [ ] Four elevations (front, rear, left, right) for bathroom addition
- [ ] Wall sections if framing changes involved

### Plumbing Plans
- [ ] Floor plan overlay showing location of ALL plumbing fixtures
- [ ] **DWV isometric/riser diagram** — all drain, waste, vent pipes with sizes, slopes (min 1/4"/ft), trap configs, vent terminations
- [ ] **Water supply diagram** — hot + cold lines with pipe sizes
- [ ] **Fixture schedule** — manufacturer and model for every fixture (toilets, sinks, tubs, showers, water heater)
- [ ] Rough-in height details — how each fixture connects to supply + drain
- [ ] Water heater detail — location, expansion tank, T&P relief valve drain
- [ ] Vent termination detail — roof penetration, clearance from windows/doors (10 ft or 3 ft above)
- [ ] Low-flow fixture compliance notation (CA Senate Bill 407)

### Supplemental Documents (2 sets)
- [ ] Drainage patterns (before + after, showing slope away from structure)
- [ ] Soils report + B-64 *(if new foundation for bathroom addition)*
- [ ] Sewer certification *(if property is in Leucadia Sewer District)*
- [ ] Storm water Form E-29 Tier 1 *(required until final occupancy)*
- [ ] Construction Waste Management Plan via Green Halo *(if project qualifies — typically ≥ 1,000 sf or $100k)*

## Step 4 — Valuation Estimate (rough)

| Scope Item | Rough Valuation Range |
|---|---|
| Bathroom addition (new room) | $20,000 – $40,000 |
| Full kitchen remodel | $20,000 – $50,000 |
| Full bath remodel | $10,000 – $25,000 |
| Re-pipe (whole house) | $8,000 – $20,000 |
| New fixtures (misc) | $2,000 – $5,000 |
| **Total estimate** | **$60,000 – $140,000+** |

Use ICC Building Valuation Data or actual contract price — whichever is **higher**.

---

## Step 7 — Inspection Sequence (Plumbing)

1. **Underground plumbing** — before any backfill (if re-pipe hits below slab)
2. **Rough plumbing** — before wall closure
   - DWV + supply piping visible and verified
   - Pressure test: water lines at 50 psi / 15 min; DWV at 5 psi or 10" mercury / 15 min
   - Fire-blocking at penetrations
3. **Final plumbing** — after all fixtures installed and operational
   - Flow, drainage, shut-off valves, vent performance

**Schedule inspections:** 760-438-3891 before 2:00 pm the working day prior, or CMI@carlsbadca.gov
