⚙️ Design Basis (Certain) — Base Zone Pathway: 5-Story Type III-A, ~14,000 SF GBA

  • Structure: Type III-A, 5 stories over concrete podium slab at grade. Floors 2–5: wood-frame light construction. Ground floor: masonry/concrete commercial shell (Group B). Total GBA ~14,000 SF (FAR 2.0 × 7,039 SF lot).
  • Party walls (shared units): 6-inch nominal double-stud party wall — 2×4 studs each side, 1" air gap, double layer 5/8" Type X gypsum each side = 2-hour fire rating, STC 55+ (UL U905). Required between all R-2 units per IBC §420.
  • Podium slab (Type III-A): 6" reinforced concrete slab at ground level, 2-hour fire-rated per IBC Table 722.5.2(5). Serves as fire barrier separating commercial (B) from residential (R-2) above.
  • Exterior walls at 0-ft side setbacks: Masonry (CMU) or 2-hr rated light-frame per IBC Table 602. 0% openings at property lines.
  • Lot merger: Lots 7 & 8 combined = 7,039 SF, 49' × 144'. Single building footprint across merged lot.
  • Sprinklers: NFPA 13 throughout all floors. Single riser (no alley-side dead end); FDC on El Cajon Blvd frontage within 100 ft of hydrant.
  • Cost basis: SD 2026 Type III-A mixed-use 5-story benchmark: $400–$460/SF. Applied $430/SF blended; commercial ground-floor shell $320/SF.
