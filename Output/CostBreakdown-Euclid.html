⚙️ Design Basis (Certain)

  • Per Review 2 drawings (04/13/26): 4 building blocks in attached townhouse row (6'-7 3/4" gaps between blocks), 2 stories, footprint ~3,422 SF × 2 = ~6,845 SF GBA. FAR ~1.10 of 2.0 max.
  • Program: 1st floor: commercial 640.38 SF (front, 30' deep) + 4 residential units (568.59 SF each) + 2 storage (33.49 SF each) + trash enclosure (196.81 SF). 2nd floor: 4 residential units (568.59 SF each) + 1 residential unit (669.80 SF).
  • Structure: Type V-A wood frame, 2×6 studs at 16" o/c, 2 stories over grade-level slab-on-grade. No basement.
  • Party walls (between the 4 blocks): 6-inch nominal double-stud party wall assembly — 2×4 studs each side, 1" air gap, double layer 5/8" Type X gypsum each side = 2-hour fire rating, STC 55+ (UL U905 or equal).
  • Exterior walls at 0-ft setback: 2×6 framing, 5/8" Type X gypsum interior + 5/8" Type X sheathing exterior, 1-hour rated, 0% openings per IBC Table 602.
  • Commercial overhang soffit (5-ft cantilever over driveway): 5/8" Type X gypsum board on metal hat channel — 1-hour rated assembly per UL L528, no unprotected penetrations.
  • Floor/ceiling assembly (1 upper floor): TJI joists, 3/4" T&G subfloor, blown-in insulation, 5/8" Type X gypsum ceiling — IIC 50+ / STC 50+ per CBC §1206.
  • Sprinklers: NFPA 13 throughout all 4 blocks + commercial unit. Separate riser per block.
  • Foundations: Continuous concrete perimeter footings, 18" wide × 12" deep, grade beam at party walls. Slab-on-grade 4" with #3 rebar at 18" o/c.
  • Parking: 1 covered stall (not required per PSTPA; provided per design).
  • Cost basis: SD 2026 hard cost benchmark — Type V-A mixed-use residential $290–$320/SF (2-story is toward lower end vs 3-story). Applied $305/SF blended for 9 resi units; $270/SF for commercial shell.
